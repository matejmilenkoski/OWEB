INTRODUCING THE M8

Performance is at the heart of the M8 Coupe. Go from smooth to sporty with standard Adaptive M Suspension and Active M Differential driving dynamics – providing the tools you need for every driving scenario. Intelligent M xDrive adjusts power distribution with distinct 4WD, 4WD Sport, and 2WD modes. Standard 6-piston front M compound brakes connect to BMW’s Integrated Brake System – providing adjustable responsiveness. Black or red calipers complete the sport look of the M8 Coupe, and are also available with gold-calipered Carbon Ceramic brakes. The M8 Competition Coupe's 4.4-liter V-8 engine delivers 617 horsepower and features an advanced cooling system, plus a uniquely designed dual oil pan that maintains oil pressure during intense maneuvers. An 8-speed M Sport Automatic Transmission takes you from smooth to aggressive with the three-stage Drivelogic shifting system. Opt for the M gearshift paddles for manual shifting.
